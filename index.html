Our story

Starbucks was founded in 1971 in Seattle, USA. It started as a small store selling coffee beans and grew into one of the largest coffee companies in the world. Starbucks is known for offering high-quality coffee, carefully sourced from different countries. The company focuses on good taste, consistent quality, and a comfortable experience for customers. Today, Starbucks is a global brand recognized for its premium coffee and strong commitment to quality.
